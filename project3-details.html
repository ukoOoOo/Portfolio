Budget Buddy

Technologies Used: React Native, Firebase, Expo

Budget Buddy is an intuitive mobile app designed to help users take control of their finances effortlessly. The app provides a seamless experience for managing personal finances with real-time updates. By automating many aspects of financial tracking, it eliminates the need for manual input, saving time and effort. With features that categorize transactions and deliver detailed statistical analysis, Budget Buddy offers users valuable insights into their spending habits, enabling smarter financial decisions.
