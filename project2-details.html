NOMAD MANAGER

A CRM solution that works alongside AI to guide nomads in Qatar

This project proposes an AI-enhanced Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system designed for Qatar's tourism sector. Aimed at improving customer interaction management, the system leverages AI technologies such as machine learning and predictive analytics to enhance decision-making and operational efficiency. Using data from web scraping, Kaggle datasets, and synthetic data generation, the AI CRM enables customer profiling and segmentation for personalized marketing campaigns. With a React-based user interface, Flask-powered backend, and secure data access protocols, the system aligns with Qatar's Vision 2030, contributing to the country's economic growth by optimizing tourism sector operations.
